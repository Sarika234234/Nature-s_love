The Lost Beauty: The Franklin Tree

The Franklin Tree (Franklinia alatamaha) was last seen in the wild in the 1800s. It was saved by botanists and is now only found in gardens.

🌱 The Ghost Orchid: A Vanishing Mystery

The Ghost Orchid (Dendrophylax lindenii) is one of the rarest orchids, known for its ghostly white petals and extreme difficulty to cultivate.
